# ⚖️ Ethical & Legal Disclosure

## Overview

All penetration testing techniques, tools, and exploits documented in this repository were performed **exclusively in isolated, controlled lab environments** against intentionally vulnerable machines. No real-world systems, networks, or services were targeted at any point.

---

## Legal Statement

- All activities documented here comply with applicable local, state, and federal laws.
- No systems were accessed without full ownership and control.
- No real credentials, personal data, or production systems were used or impacted.
- All testing was conducted in compliance with the terms of service of any third-party platforms used (e.g., TryHackMe, HackTheBox).

---

## Responsible Use Notice

The techniques and tools demonstrated in this repository are documented **for educational purposes only** as part of a cybersecurity portfolio. They are intended to show understanding of offensive security concepts so that defenders can better protect real systems.

**These techniques must never be used against:**
- Any system you do not personally own
- Any system you do not have **explicit, written authorization** to test
- Any live production system or network
- Any third-party service or infrastructure
